# Aurad0ck Business DNA & Landing Page Content

## Product Name: Aurad0ck
**Tagline:** The invisible storage expansion for your MacBook Pro.

## Core Mission
To provide professional MacBook users (video editors, developers, photographers) with a seamless, high-capacity storage solution that integrates directly into the laptop's form factor, eliminating the need for dangling external drives.

## Key Features
- **Integrated Design:** Replaces or snaps onto the bottom shell of the MacBook Pro.
- **Dual Storage:** Supports 1x M.2 NVMe SSD (high speed) and optional support for 2.5" SSD (high capacity).
- **Flush Connectivity:** Connects via lateral USB-C/Thunderbolt ports with ultra-low profile connectors.
- **Thermal Management:** Aluminum construction acts as a giant heatsink for the SSDs and the Mac.
- **Kickstarter Ready:** Designed for community-backed industrial production.

## Target Audience
- Creative professionals (4K/8K video editing).
- Data scientists & Developers.
- Digital nomads who need large local storage without the bulk.

## Visual Identity (for Image Generation)
- **Materials:** CNC-machined aluminum, Space Gray and Silver finishes.
- **Aesthetic:** Minimalist, industrial, "Apple-native" look.
- **Detail:** Subtle laser-etched logo, micro-LED status indicator.
